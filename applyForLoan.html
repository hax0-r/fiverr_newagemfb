We make the process of accessing loans very seamless. Please note, we can only give you a loan if you have an account with us that has been running for at least 6 months.

SALARY
ACCOUNT

REQUIREMENTS

  •   Loan Application Letter.
  •   Letter of introduction from the employer.
  •   Letter of domiciliation of monthly salary.
  •   Physical evidence of the loan purpose.
  •   Two salary current account holder guarantors.
  •   3% mandatory deposit.
  •   Credit bureau report.

START
APPLICATION

By submitting this information you give NEWAGE MICROFINANCE BANK permission to keep your details and use same to verify your claims, contact you and process your loan request.
